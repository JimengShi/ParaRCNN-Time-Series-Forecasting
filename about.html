Introduction

My name is Jimeng Shi, a CS Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Giri Narasimhan from Knight Foundation School of Computing and Information Sciences at Florida International University. My research focus is to address various real-world problems by developing machine learning tools to obtain accurate perceptron and generate effective decisions. Recently, I have been working on a flood prediction and mitigation project using AI models. My work is being funded by NSF I-GUIDE.
